About Our Multipitch Services

Our guided multipitch climbing trips take place in some of the most beautiful and awe-inspiring locations in the region. Our experienced guides will take you on a journey that will test your limits and push you to new heights.

We offer a variety of trips for advanced climbers, including challenging routes that will take you to some of the highest peaks in the region. Our guides are knowledgeable about the terrain and will ensure that you have a safe and unforgettable experience.

We provide all necessary gear, including ropes, harnesses, helmets, and climbing shoes. You will need to bring appropriate clothing and food for the trip, as well as a positive attitude and a willingness to push yourself.
